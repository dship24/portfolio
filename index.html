--------------- About Me ---------------

I am currently a student at Base Camp Coding Academy, where I am training to become a full-stack developer. My aspirations to become a software developer began in my senior year of high school.

I enjoy software development because it allows me to be creative and bring new ideas to life. I am particularly interested in back-end programming with languages like Python and Java; however, I am open to learning new skills to expand my technical "arsenal".
